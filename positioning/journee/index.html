    • Our Purpose

    Our purpose is to make traveling simple and easy by giving you everything you need to know about where you'll be going on your next vacation. We are happy to invite you and others who are interested in traveling the world, Ages 21-70 to utilize the quick and easy way to plan your next vacation.

    Why Us?

    We want to help world travelers with any level of experience achieve their dreams by giving them opportunities to learn about different cultures and provide them with the knowledge of foods, music, activities and traditional clothing to help prepare them with their journee around the world. People will want to come to Journee because they are looking for a special and exciting memory they would want to keep forever. They want to experience a different part of the world; they want to know more about the cultures and the many different lifestyles and do it without anything to worry about.
