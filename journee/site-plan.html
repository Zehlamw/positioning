Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to make traveling simple and easy by giving you everything you need to know about where you'll be going on your next vacation.

Audience

Our target customers are to all those interested in traveling the world. Ages 21-65. Many know their way around technology and will be traveling and will use primarily mobile devices to access the site.

Content

Home page

We want to help world travelers with any level of experience achieve their dreams by giving them opportunities to learn about different cultures and provide them with the knowledge of foods, music, activities and traditional clothing to help prepare them with their journey around the world. People will want to come to my website because they are looking for a special, and exciting memory they would want to keep forever. They want to experience a different part of the world; they want to know more about the cultures and the many different lifestyles and do it all without anything to worry about.

Images for the Home page

Culture Education

Learn about where you’ll be going. Discover the culture and get a taste of what it’s like to live their lifestyle with food, activities, music, and food that they provide.

Images for the Page 2

Explore

Most people worry about their budget and what they can fit into it while on vacation, some may ask, where can I find great food and what is there around that is fun that I can fill my time with? Let me tell you that all you need to prepare is your budget and your destination. We will take care of the rest! Let us know where you will be going and we will provide you with great food choices, activities, give you a hint of the music you might hear or clothing that the local people will be wearing. We have it all and it’s all for you!
